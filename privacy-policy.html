Privacy Policy

At Focus Academy, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or enroll in our German language courses. By using our services, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you register for our German language courses or consult our travel services, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details.
  • Non-Personal Information: We may also gather non-personal information, such as browser type, IP address, and browsing behavior, to enhance your user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process your enrollment in our German language programs and manage your account.
  • To communicate important information regarding your courses, including schedules and updates.
  • To provide customer support and address inquiries related to our courses and services.
  • To improve our website and deliver a more personalized learning experience.
  • To send promotional materials about our language courses and travel consultation services if you opt in to receive them.

Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • To trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, as long as they agree to keep this information confidential.
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights and those of our users.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and request corrections to your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for the processing of your data at any time.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the revised policy on our website. Your continued use of our services after any changes indicates your acceptance of the new policy.
